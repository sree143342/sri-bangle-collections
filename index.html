About Us

Sri Bangle Collections is dedicated to bringing you exquisite, handcrafted bangles that blend traditional artistry with modern elegance.

We focus on quality, creativity, and personalized customer service to ensure every piece tells a unique story.

We offer an exceptional range of Kundan designs, and also create custom pieces tailored to your unique preferences.

Our collection extends beyond bangles to include a variety of Kundan ornaments such as earrings, hair bands, necklaces, and hair clips — each piece crafted to reflect grace, artistry, and luxury.
